Causes, symptoms and treatment of breast cancer

You have the opportunity to get acquainted in detail with the information of Karima Chuliyeva, Oncogynecologist of the Higher Category of the National Medical Center about the causes, symptoms and treatment of breast cancer through this article.

The reasons:
1. Hormonal factors: High levels of estrogen and progesterone, shorter periods of pregnancy and breastfeeding.
2. Turn-free style: Smoking, alcohol consumption, poor diet, and low mobility also increase the risk.

Characters:
Some common symptoms of breast cancer include:
- Pain or burning sensation in the chest (swelling)
- Changes in breast shape or size
- Tingling of the skin or thickening of the tissues
- Discharge of blood or fluid from the nipple

Diagnosis:
It is important to regularly examine the breast for detection in the lower stages.
1. Mammography: With the help of Rengen, changes in the breast are detected.
2. Ultrasonography: To confirm tumors or learn more about its nature.
3. Biopsy: Samples are taken from the mass and examined in a laboratory setting.

Treatment:
Treatment methods are prescribed depending on the stage of the disease:
- Surgery: Part of the tumor or the entire breast can be removed.
- Chemotherapy: Chemicals are used to kill cancer cells.
- Radiotherapy: Exposure to the tumor with radiation.
- Hormonal therapy: Stopping the development of tumors by regulating hormone levels.

Prevention:
The possibility of early detection and treatment of breast cancer can significantly improve its outcome. For this reason, regular preventive examinations are important.
